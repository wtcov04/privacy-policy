Data Deletion Instructions for SocialScope

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from SocialScope, please follow these steps:

1. Request Account Deletion

Email us at wt.cov@hotmail.com with the subject line "Request Data Deletion". In your email, include your account details such as your username or email address associated with the account.

2. Data Deletion Process

Once we receive your request, we will process it within [Insert Number] business days. Your account data will be permanently deleted from our servers.

3. Confirmation

You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been successfully deleted.

4. Data Retention Period

We retain user data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Once your data is deleted, it will no longer be accessible to you or us.
