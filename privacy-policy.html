Privacy Policy for SocialScope

At SocialScope, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our web application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use SocialScope, we collect the following information:

  • Authentication Information: We use OAuth to authenticate you via your Instagram or TikTok accounts. This allows us to retrieve public social media data, such as follower counts, engagement metrics, and posts.
  • User Profile Data: When you create an account, we collect your email and password to store securely in Firebase.

2. How We Use the Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide you with personalized social media insights, including follower growth, engagement rates, and post-performance.
  • Display your Instagram or TikTok profile data within the SocialScope dashboard.
  • Send you notifications about your account activity and updates to our service.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for data processing:

  • Instagram API: We use the Instagram API to fetch your Instagram profile data and analytics.
  • TikTok API: We use the TikTok API for similar data retrieval and analytics.
  • Firebase: We use Firebase for authentication and secure storage of your personal information.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption techniques to protect your personal data. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time by disconnecting your social media accounts from SocialScope.
  • Request a copy of the data we store on you by contacting us directly.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
